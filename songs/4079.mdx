## English Translation

O Lord, my eyes are searching for You
day and night.

In light and darkness, in home and
outside, within mind it is possible to hold You.
You never hate any one.
You do not expect
anything from anyone.
You are helpful to all. You are ocean of kindness.
You are resort of
helpless in life and death.

I have relationship with since eras after eras.
I am obedient
to You from beginning till end.
Never forget me, never go away far, I have come into Your
shelter, O the Great Creation, Maha鈥檚ambhu鈥檛i.

O Lord, my eyes are searching for You
day and night.
